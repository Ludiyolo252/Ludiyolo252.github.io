A bingo card to fill out while watching Aia Amare from NIJISANJI EN.

Mark a spot whenever something matching one of your squares happens on stream! Please be aware, refreshing the website randomizes your bingo card again.
Also, you cannot untick a square, so be careful! If you get a bingo, you can share it on Twitter (X) with #bingoob. Have fun playing!
